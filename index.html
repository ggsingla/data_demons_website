December 2018

Journey started

The idea of this NGO was given by: Late. Smt. Jolly Jhanji. This NGO is currently being operated by her better-half Sh. Harsh Jhanji. She loved to help people all around her which encouraged her to go forward with the idea of this NGO. She passed away in a very small age of just 30 yrs but her works made her immortal among all of us and this website is a project made in the memory of her great works.

We are proud say that the NGO has remained non-profitable since the day of its commencement
